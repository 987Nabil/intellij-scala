Reports comparing Scala collections of different categories.

Scala collections cannot be equal if they are of different categories (Set, Seq, Map or Iterator). Comparing them with arrays is also pointless. This inspection suggests to convert one of the collections to make them of the same kind.

Example:


  seq == iterator
  seq != array

After the quick-fix is applied:


  seq.toIterator == iterator or seq == iterator.toSeq
  seq != array.toSeq
