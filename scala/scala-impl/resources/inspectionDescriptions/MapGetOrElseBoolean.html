Reports map and getOrElse(false) / getOrElse(true) and suggests replacing them with exists / forall.

Example:


  def p(x: Int): Boolean = ???
  Option(0).map(p).getOrElse(false)
  Option(0).map(p).getOrElse(true)

After the quick-fix is applied:


  def p(x: Int): Boolean = ???
  Option(0).exists(p)
  Option(0).forall(p)
